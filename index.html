Quasistetic Media

I found I could say things with color and shapes that I couldn't say any other way—things I had no words for. Whatever I can't turn into something wonderful, I let loose. Truth does not excite me. I only engage in drunkenness and euphoria, and when daily existence binds me, I flee in some manner. There will be no more walls.
